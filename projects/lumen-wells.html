lumen wells

Spring 2025 | Design VI

Gabriela Garza & Hannah Ongmanchi

Situated at the intersection of MLK Boulevard and Waller Creek, Lumen Wells is a proposed biomedical research facility for the University of Texas that investigates the evolving relationship between science and architecture. The design responds to the extremes of its surrounding environment while reexamining the spatial and technical standards that define the contemporary laboratory.
